How do I book a taxi with SOT Travels in Coimbatore? +
You can book a taxi by calling us on +91 94888 40240 or sending your trip details on WhatsApp. Share your pickup location, date, time, destination, number of passengers and vehicle type (Sedan, SUV, Traveller etc.) and we will confirm with a clear quote.
How are the kilometres and hours calculated for outstation trips? +
For most outstation trips from Coimbatore, kilometres are calculated as garage to garage (starting from vehicle starting point until return). Packages will clearly mention included km and hours. Extra km and extra hours are charged as per the rate card shared in advance.
What are the additional charges I should be aware of? +
Toll, parking, interstate permit charges and entry fees are usually extra and payable by the customer as per actuals, unless mentioned as included in a package. For outstation night halt, driver bata / allowance will be clearly mentioned in the quote.
Do you offer customized tour packages from Coimbatore? +
Yes. Along with fixed Ooty, Kodaikanal, Munnar and Kerala tour packages, SOT Travels also designs fully customized itineraries based on your number of days, budget, preferred locations and type of group (family, friends, corporate or pilgrimage).
